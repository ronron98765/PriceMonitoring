About APOO
Agricultural Productivity Operations Office · Cagayan de Oro City

The Agricultural Productivity Operations Office (APOO) of Cagayan de Oro City is the local government unit mandated to promote, develop, and regulate agriculture and fisheries since 1961 — ensuring food security and the welfare of farmers and fisherfolk in the City of Golden Friendship.

APOO provides extension services, technical assistance, market information, and direct support programs to more than 18,000 registered farmers and fisherfolk across all 80 barangays of the city.

Vision

A food-secure and prosperous Cagayan de Oro with resilient and sustainable agriculture and fisheries sectors.

Mission

To provide effective support services enabling equitable and sustainable agri-fishery development for the city's farming and fishing communities.
